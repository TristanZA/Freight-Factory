Estate Agents Earn More With Freight Factory

Tristan Wiggill

Apart from buying or selling homes, your clients may wish to import, export or relocate their vehicles or other high-value personal goods. 

When you refer these clients to Freight Factory and they make use of our world-class shipping services, we pay you a handsome commission, no questions asked!

The best part is, you don’t have to worry about anything - Freight Factory takes the hassle out of shipping cars, motorcycles, caravans and other high-value personal items into, out of and across South Africa.

You don't have to lift a finger!

We facilitate all aspects of shipping, be it full or shared container loads. We do all required export/import documentation. And we even offer comprehensive marine insurance. 

Our warehouses and loading facilities are based within secure business parks, giving your client's discretion and the peace of mind they deserve whilst we handle their precious assets.

Freight Factory’s operation is tightly controlled with well-trained, in-house staff. Only the best tried and tested suppliers and destination agents are used. 

All required export documents are done by our traffic official. We handle  import applications, ie. Import ITAC Permits, LOA’s, TIP’s, Carnets, Importers Codes and Rebates.

Vehicles are loaded at our warehouse by our own staff. If the vehicle requires local / national relocation, we arrange this too. We can also view/vet vehicles on behalf of your clients that do not reside in South Africa. 

All vehicles are parked indoors in secure business parks with controlled access. Full CCTV cameras are linked to armed response. 

Our extensive warehouses in Johannesburg and Cape Town accommodate all types of vehicles, from the smallest to the largest. 

All vehicles stored with us until shipping, receive a car cover and, if needed, we can trickle charge batteries too. 

Vehicles being shipped with us get up to three months free storage!

On arrival in either of our warehouses, full inspection checks sheets with photographs are completed and compared to the road haulers/client pre-trip check sheet.  

Inspections with sellers puts both buyers and sellers at ease when purchasing vehicles abroad.  Full, detailed checks are done on each vehicle inspected. 

Depending on what your client requires, we can either supply extensive photographs or Facetime your client, walking them around the vehicle in real-time.

All vehicle paperwork is checked and validated prior to any monies changing hands, leaving no surprises when shipping commences.

If required, monies can be paid into a holding bank account and, once given the go ahead from the buyer, be released to the seller who takes receipt thereof.

Once we are satisfied that the vehicle is in the same condition as when it left the seller’s premises, will we sign off receipt of the vehicle.

You’ve worked hard to sell your client’s home, now let Freight Factory take care of all their shipping needs!
